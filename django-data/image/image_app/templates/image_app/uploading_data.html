Uploading data into BioSamples

The following steps illustrate the procedure to input a dataset into the Biosample archive, thus subsequently making it available on the IMAGE portal

Signing Up and registering to EBI AAP

Click on Sign-Up in the right-upper corner of InjectTool site and start to fill the form. You have to provide a valid email address and select your organization from the provided list. If you don't find your organization, you have to contact InjectTool developers.

After registration is complete, you will receive a mail to the address used for registration. Click on the activation link, in order to complete InjectTool registration. Now you will require an EBI AAP profile in order to submiy your data into biosample. InjectTool is able to do such registration. However, we can't store your AAP credentials in our site. You need to keep track of them, otherwise you will not be able to submit your data to biosample. Click on Generate a new AAP profile button a fill out the form by proving a strong password to create a new EBI AAP profile

If the registration succeded, you will generate a new AAP token. This token is stored in your browser session and will be used to perform biosample submission. The token will be valid for one hour. When submitting data to biosample, InjectTool will check token validity and will ask to the user to generate a new token if necessary. You can check your token status by cliking on My Token on your username dropdown list in the right upper side of the page
