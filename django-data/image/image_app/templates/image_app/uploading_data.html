Signing Up and registering to EBI AAP

Click on Sign-Up in the right-upper corner of InjectTool site and start to fill the form. You have to provide a valid email address and select your organization from the provided list. If you don't find your organization, you have to contact InjectTool developers.

After registration is complete, you will receive a mail to the address used for registration. Click on the activation link, and follow the instructions to create a new EBI AAP account
